## FAQ

**Q: Do I need special equipment?**
A: Just a device with internet and a microphone. Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

**Q: Is Pray.so denominational?**
A: We welcome all Christian denominations. Create rooms that fit your tradition.

**Q: Can I pray silently?**
A: Absolutely. You can listen and type prayers in chat without speaking.

**Q: How do you prevent abuse?**
A: Hosts can mute, remove participants, and report issues. Our team reviews reports within hours.

**Q: What if my internet is slow?**
A: Our audio is optimized for low bandwidth. You can join with as little as 100kbps.

**Q: Can I record sessions?**
A: Premium users can record their own hosted rooms. Respect and permission are required.
